                                            <h1>Printing Labels</h1>

                                <br/>

                                            <p><strong>This program only needs to be installed on the Windows computer where the label printer is physically connected.</strong></p>
                                            <p>
                                                The ChemSW NBT platform uses a Windows program to generate labels using your local label printer. The Label Printer Client (LPC) program makes this printer available for selection by anyone logged into your ChemSW Live customer database. As long as this program is left running on the label printer computer(s), labels will be printed quickly and efficiently.
                                            </p>
                                            <h2>Installation Steps</h2>
                                            <p>
                                                <h3>#1- <a href="NbtPrintClient/NbtPrintClient.zip">Download the LPC Installation ZIP here</a>.</h3>
                                                <ul>
                                                    <li>You may receive an installation security warning from your browser, click OK/Accept.</li>
                                                    <li>Locate the downloaded ZIP (archive) and extract it, then run SETUP.EXE.</li>
                                                    <li>Follow the Installer instructions and choose Everyone for easiest use</li>
                                                </ul>
                                                <br/><img src="NbtPrintClient/install1.jpg"/> 
                                                <br/><img src="NbtPrintClient/install2.jpg"/> 
                                                <br/><img src="NbtPrintClient/install3.jpg"/> 
                                                <br/><img src="NbtPrintClient/install4.jpg"/> 
                                            </p>
                                            <p>
                                                
                                                <h3>#2- Run the ChemSW Label Printer Client from your desktop or Start Menu by clicking the icon</h3>
                                                <br/><img src="NbtPrintClient/install5.jpg"/>
                                                <br/><img src="NbtPrintClient/install6.jpg"/> 
                                            </p>
                                            <p>                                        
                                                <h3>#3 - Click the Setup tab, and select your label printer</h3>
                                                <br/><img src="NbtPrintClient/install7.jpg"/> 
                                                <br/><img src="NbtPrintClient/install8.jpg"/> 
                                                <br/><img src="NbtPrintClient/install9.jpg"/> 
                                            </p>
                                            <p>    
                                                <h3>#4 - Provide <em>login credentials</em> for ChemSW Live.</h3>
                                                <br/><img src="NbtPrintClient/install13a.jpg"/> 
                                            </p>
                                            <p>    
                                                <h3>#5- Provide a <em>unique name</em> for your printer and click <em>Register</em>.</h3> This name will be displayed inside the ChemSW Live software. Remember: there may be many printers defined for your company, so create a name that helps users understand where this printer is located.
                                                <br/><img src="NbtPrintClient/install11.jpg"/> 
                                                <p>If the name is unique, you should see a Success message like this (PrinterKey will differ):</p>
                                                <br/><img src="NbtPrintClient/install12.jpg"/> 

                                            </p>
                                            <p>    
                                                <h3>#6 - Now that a printer is registered, check the <em>Enable Print Jobs</em> box.</h3>
                                                <br/><img src="NbtPrintClient/install13b.jpg"/> 
                                            </p>

                                            <p>    
                                                <h3>#7- Save your settings.</h3>
                                                <br/>
                                                Your printer is now waiting for a label print job from ChemSW Live. You can log into ChemSW Live and try printing a barcode label to this printer.
                                                <br/><img src="NbtPrintClient/install14.jpg"/>
                                                <h3>You are done!</h3> 
                                            </p>

                                            <h2>Changing the ChemSW Printer Name</h2>
                                <p>
                                    If you decide to change your printer name in the future, be aware that the print job queue for this printer may not be completed. An administrator can modify the existing Print Jobs to send them to a different printer from inside the ChemSW hosted application (Printers View, Print Jobs tab).
                                </p>
                              
                                                <h3>First- Click the Setup Tab</h3>
                                                <img src="NbtPrintClient/install20.jpg"/>
                                                <h3>Next- Click Clear</h3>
                                                <img src="NbtPrintClient/install21.jpg"/>
                                                <h3>Then- Click OK to the warning</h3>
                                                <img src="NbtPrintClient/install22.jpg"/>
                                            <h3>Finally, you can now provide a new printer name and click Register:</h3>
                                                <img src="NbtPrintClient/install11.jpg"/>

                                            <h2>Debugging Your Printer</h2>
                                <p>
                                    You can test whether your label printer is working (after you have selected it at step #3) using the Test tab.
                                                <br/><img src="NbtPrintClient/install30.jpg"/>
                                                <br/> Clicking Print EPL will generate a standard test label on the selected printer.
                                </p>
